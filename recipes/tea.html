TEA

One of the most consumed hot drinks in the world

INCREDIENTS

  • Milk
  • Water
  • Tea Powder
  • Sugar

STEPS

  1. Mix Equal amounts of milk and water and keep to boil
  2. Once the milk rises up, lower the flame and add tea powder as required.
  3. Let the essence sink in. Keep boiling in lower flame for a while
  4. Filter the tea so as to remove the tea powder at the bottom
  5. Add sugar to taste
