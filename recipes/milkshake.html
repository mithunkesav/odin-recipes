SHARJA SHAKE

Sharja shake is a kind of milkshake found southern parts of India

INCREDIENTS

  • Milk
  • Plantain
  • Sugar
  • Boost/Horlicks/Bourvita
  • Cashew/Badam

STEPS

  1. Break deep freezed ice using a stick or something similar until the consistency is loose
  2. Take a mixer jar and move the milk to the jar
  3. add sugar as required
  4. add bournvita
  5. add plantain
  6. add cashew or badam
    7. Mix the entire thing until the remaining becomes smooth and drinkable.
